About Me

I am an Engineering Honours student at Swinburne University, majoring in Software. My main interests lie in low-level computing and Linux scripting, and I enjoy running.

I use a MacBook Pro as my main computer, primarily for its UNIX-like terminal and impressive 20-hour battery life. On my home server, which I host myself, I run NixOS for its extensive flexibility, declarative configuration, and simple rollback features. My system dotfiles.

I am greatly inspired by the Suckless Manifesto.
